# Appreciative Inquiry (AI) 4-D Cycle

* **Best for:** Designing controls based on "what good looks like" rather than just fixing problems (focusing on strengths).
* **Steps:**
  * **Discover:** Finding the bright spots and success stories.
  * **Dream:** Imagining the ideal future state.
  * **Design:** Creating the systems to make the dream a reality.
  * **Destiny:** Committing to action and sustaining the change.
* **Sentence Starters:**
  * *Discover:* "When has work felt mentally healthy here? Tell those stories." / "Tell me about a time when the team worked really well together."
  * *Dream:* "If those good days were the norm, what would this place be like?" / "Imagine we solved this perfectly; what does it look like?"
  * *Design:* "What systems and behaviours would make that normal?" / "How do we build that into our daily routine?"
  * *Destiny:* "What will we commit to trial over the next 3 months?" / "How will we keep this alive?"
* **Sentence Follow-ups:**
  * *Discover:* "What made that possible?"
  * *Dream:* "How would that feel different to today?"
  * *Design:* "What barriers do we need to remove?"
  * *Destiny:* "Who is going to champion this?"
* **Avoid:**
  * **Toxic Positivity.** Acknowledge pain before pivoting to the positive.
    * *Example:* Avoid "Let's focus on the good stuff!" when people are hurting; try "I hear how hard it is right now. When things *were* working well, what was different?"
  * **Silencing complaints.**
    * *Example:* Avoid "No negative talk today"; try "Let's use that frustration to design a better way."
