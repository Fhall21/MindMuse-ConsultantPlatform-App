  ### **CLEAR (Clarify, Listen, Explore, Action, Review)**

* **Best for:** Structured support meetings or consults to keep them on track. (Note: Originally "Contract", updated to "Clarify" to sound less formal).  
* **Steps:**  
  * **Clarify:** Agreeing on the focus and time boundaries.  
  * **Listen:** Gathering the full story.  
  * **Explore:** Deepening understanding of drivers and impacts.  
  * **Action:** Co-designing practical solutions.  
  * **Review:** Setting accountability and follow-up.  
* **Sentence Starters:**  
  * *Clarify:* "What would be most useful for us to focus on today?" / "We have 30 minutes; what's the priority?"  
  * *Listen:* "Tell me what's been happening."  
  * *Explore:* "What impact is this having on your work and home life?" / "What do you think is driving this?"  
  * *Action:* "What are 1-3 practical things we can change?"  
  * *Review:* "What will you try, and when should we check back in?"  
* **Sentence Follow-ups:**  
  * *Clarify:* "Is that realistic for today?"  
  * *Listen:* "And then what happened?"  
  * *Explore:* "How does that sit with you?"  
  * *Action:* "Is that doable?"  
  * *Review:* "How will we know if it worked?"  
* **Avoid:**  
  * **Aimless chatting.**  
    * *Example:* Avoid spending 25 minutes on small talk and 5 minutes on the problem; try "I want to make sure we have time to solve X, let's dive in."
