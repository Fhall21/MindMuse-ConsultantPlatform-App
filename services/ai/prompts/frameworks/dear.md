### **DEAR (Describe, Express, Assert, Reinforce)**

* **Best for:** Asserting boundaries or addressing performance issues respectfully without aggression.  
* **Steps:**  
  * **Describe:** Stating the facts neutrally without judgement.  
  * **Express:** Sharing feelings/concerns using "I" statements.  
  * **Assert:** Stating the request or boundary clearly.  
  * **Reinforce:** Explaining the positive outcome or mutual benefit.  
* **Sentence Starters:**  
  * *Describe:* "I noticed the report was submitted 2 days late." / "We agreed on a deadline of Tuesday."  
  * *Express:* "I felt stressed because the team deadline was impacted." / "I am concerned that the client will be unhappy."  
  * *Assert:* "I need updates ahead of deadlines if something will be delayed." / "I need you to arrive on time."  
  * *Reinforce:* "That way, we can adjust plans and avoid last-minute pressure." / "This will help the team trust you."  
* **Sentence Follow-ups:**  
  * *Describe:* "Is that your understanding too?"  
  * *Express:* "It makes it hard for me to plan."  
  * *Assert:* "Can you agree to that?"  
  * *Reinforce:* "It's a win-win."  
* **Avoid:**  
  * **Aggressive language.**  
    * *Example:* Avoid "You are always late"; try "This is the third time you have been late."  
  * **Being passive.**  
    * *Example:* Avoid "It would be nice if you could..."; try "I need you to..."
