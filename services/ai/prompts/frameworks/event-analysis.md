  ### **Event Analysis (Before, During, After, Learning)**

* **Best for:** Analysing specific harmful episodes (bullying, high-pressure incidents) without blame.  
* **Steps:**  
  * **Before:** Analysing context and antecedents.  
  * **During:** Analysing actions and responses.  
  * **After:** Analysing impact and fallout.  
  * **Learning:** Planning prevention and system changes.  
* **Sentence Starters:**  
  * *Before:* "What led up to it? What was the context: demands, staffing, communication?" / "Were there warning signs?"  
  * *During:* "What happened, who was involved, and how did you try to manage it?"  
  * *After:* "What were the impacts: emotionally, physically, on work and relationships?" / "How are people feeling now?"  
  * *Learning:* "What would have helped? What needs to be different next time?" / "What system change would prevent this?"  
* **Sentence Follow-ups:**  
  * *Before:* "Was the team under pressure?"  
  * *During:* "Did the procedure work?"  
  * *After:* "Is everyone okay?"  
  * *Learning:* "Who needs to know about this?"  
* **Avoid:**  
  * **Hindsight bias.**  
    * *Example:* Avoid "You should have known"; try "What information was missing at the time?"
