  ### **GROW (Goal, Reality, Options, Will)**

* **Best for:** Coaching someone towards a solution or action plan when they are stuck.  
* **Steps:**  
  * **Goal:** Defining the desired future state.  
  * **Reality:** Exploring the current situation and constraints.  
  * **Options:** Brainstorming possibilities and alternatives.  
  * **Will:** Committing to specific actions and support.  
* **Sentence Starters:**  
  * *Goal:* "What would 'things feeling manageable' look like for you in 3 months?" / "What do you want to achieve?"  
  * *Reality:* "What's happening right now? What does a bad week look like?"  
  * *Options:* "What could change: in your work, in how it's organised, or in how we support you?" / "If there were no constraints, what would you change?"  
  * *Will:* "What's one concrete step you feel ready to take?" / "What will you do first?"  
* **Sentence Follow-ups:**  
  * *Goal:* "How will you know you've got there?"  
  * *Reality:* "What have you tried so far?"  
  * *Options:* "What else could you do?"  
  * *Will:* "What support do you need from me to do that?"  
* **Avoid:**  
  * **Leading the witness.** The power comes from their ideas.  
    * *Example:* Avoid "Why don't you try X?"; try "What options do you see?"  
  * **Clichéd metaphors.**  
    * *Example:* Avoid "If you had a magic wand..."; try "If you could change one thing..."
