  ### **Plus / Delta (Start-Stop-Continue)**

* **Best for:** Closing a session and capturing quick actions and feedback.  
* **Steps:**  
  * **Plus / Start:** Identifying positives, new ideas, or things to begin.  
  * **Delta / Stop:** Identifying improvements, harms, or things to end.  
  * **Continue:** Identifying things to protect and maintain.  
* **Sentence Starters:**  
  * *Plus / Start:* "What's working well? What should we start doing?" / "What should we do more of?"  
  * *Delta / Stop:* "What needs to change? What should we stop doing?" / "What isn't working?"  
  * *Continue:* "What is worth protecting and maintaining?" / "What should we keep doing?"  
* **Sentence Follow-ups:**  
  * *Plus / Start:* "How do we make that happen?"  
  * *Delta / Stop:* "What is the alternative?"  
  * *Continue:* "Why is that important to you?"  
* **Avoid:**  
  * **Listing "Deltas" without action owners.**  
    * *Example:* Avoid leaving a list of complaints on the whiteboard; try "Who is taking ownership of fixing item 3?"  
  * **Jargon.**  
    * *Example:* Avoid "What's the delta?"; try "What needs to change?"
