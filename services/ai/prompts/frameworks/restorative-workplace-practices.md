  ### **Restorative Workplace Practices**

* **Best for:** Repairing relationships after conflict or misconduct, focusing on healing rather than punishment.  
* **Steps:**  
  * **Perspective:** Hearing from all sides to understand the narrative.  
  * **Impact:** Understanding the harm caused to individuals and the team.  
  * **Resolution:** Agreeing on the fix and future behaviour.  
* **Sentence Starters:**  
  * *Perspective:* "What happened from your perspective?" / "Tell me your side of the story."  
  * *Impact:* "Who has been affected and how?" / "How do you think this impacted the team?"  
  * *Resolution:* "What do you think needs to happen to make things better now?" / "How can we move forward?"  
* **Sentence Follow-ups:**  
  * *Perspective:* "What were you thinking at the time?"  
  * *Impact:* "What has been the hardest part for you?"  
  * *Resolution:* "Is that something you can commit to?"  
* **Avoid:**  
  * **Forced apologies.**  
    * *Example:* Avoid "Say you're sorry"; try "What do you think X needs to hear from you?"  
  * **Using it for bullying.**  
    * *Example:* Do not use restorative circles if one party is afraid of the other; safety first.
