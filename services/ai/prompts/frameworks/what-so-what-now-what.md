  ### **"What? So what? Now what?" Reflection**

* **Best for:** Lightweight reflection for shorter toolbox talks or reviews.  
* **Steps:**  
  * **What?:** Describing the event, issue, or observation.  
  * **So what?:** Analysing the impact, meaning, and importance.  
  * **Now what?:** Planning the next steps and actions.  
* **Sentence Starters:**  
  * *What?:* "What happened / what is happening?" / "What did we notice this week?"  
  * *So what?:* "How does it affect you, others, and the organisation?" / "Why does this matter?"  
  * *Now what?:* "What should change at system, team, and individual levels?" / "What are we going to do differently tomorrow?"  
* **Sentence Follow-ups:**  
  * *What?:* "What are the facts?"  
  * *So what?:* "Is this a trend?"  
  * *Now what?:* "When do we start?"  
* **Avoid:**  
  * **Getting stuck in "What".** Reporting is not fixing.  
    * *Example:* Avoid ending the meeting after listing the problems; try "Okay, we know the problem. Now what are we doing about it?"
